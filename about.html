Hey, there

Welcome to my quaint little corner of cyberspace. This is a humble personal project where I plan to write and publish  just about whatever I feel like. On the home page you will (optimistically) find a new edition of my yet-to-be-completely-named publication that will contain the following:

 

  • A quote that I found to be especially moving or interesting
  • A word that tickles my brain or is smarmily relevant to the rest of the material
  • A new edition of my PEMDAS puzzle
  • An original longer-form piece of writing on a topic that I’ve been thinking about for a while
  • An original shorter-form piece of writing on a topic that may or may not have just crossed my path

 

I hope you enjoy your time here and have at least one Thought provoked. If you want to say hi, you can reach me here: bradley.is.h@outlook.com
