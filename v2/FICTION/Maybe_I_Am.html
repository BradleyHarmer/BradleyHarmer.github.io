Maybe I Am

May 30, 2025 · 144 words

I think about ghosts and how they feel

I think about whether or not they feel

Whether they are hungry or full or neither

Whether it depends on their last meal

 

I think about ghosts because I’d like to be one

Because are they not privileged visitors

Without the pain of expectation?

And don’t they get to rest?

 

But maybe restful death is presumptuous

Maybe words like rest have no meaning

Maybe ghosts stopped paying attention

 

But I think ghosts can feel and understand

And I think they care because they’re still watching

When they could have chosen nowhere

 

Ghosts are god without the responsibility

But I bet you they forget they aren’t quite here

And they reach out while we fall

 

I wonder about the ghosts who have tried to talk me

I wonder if I’m right

I wonder when I’ll find out
